A curated selection of podcasts, newsletters, and community websites for iOS and Swift developers.

## iOS and Swift podcasts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Swift by Sundell" icon="mic" href="https://www.swiftbysundell.com/podcast/">
    In-depth conversations about Swift development and iOS topics. Hosted by John Sundell, known for his approachable teaching style.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Under the Radar" icon="radio" href="https://www.relay.fm/radar">
    A podcast about independent iOS development. Hosted by Marco Arment and David Smith, focused on the business and craft of indie apps.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Swift Unwrapped" icon="code" href="https://spec.fm/podcasts/swift-unwrapped">
    A podcast dedicated to the Swift programming language itself, covering evolution proposals and language internals.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stacktrace" icon="layers" href="https://stacktracepodcast.fm">
    A weekly podcast about Apple news and development. Clear, concise, and developer-focused.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Fireside Swift" icon="flame" href="https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/fireside-swift/id1269435221?mt=2">
    Two experienced iOS developers discuss a single Swift or iOS topic per episode in a casual format.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Core Intuition" icon="cpu" href="http://coreint.org/">
    A podcast about indie software development for Apple platforms, hosted by Manton Reece and Daniel Jalkut.
  </Card>
  <Card title="iPhreaks" icon="phone" href="https://devchat.tv/iphreaks/">
    A weekly discussion on iOS development and adjacent topics. Panel format with rotating guests.
  </Card>
  <Card title="More Than Just Code" icon="terminal" href="https://mtjc.fireside.fm/">
    A podcast for iOS developers that covers Swift, Xcode, and the broader Apple ecosystem.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Additional podcasts:

- [The Ray Wenderlich Podcast](https://www.raywenderlich.com/podcast) — Tutorials, interviews and tips from the raywenderlich.com team.
- [Debug](https://www.imore.com/debug) — A deep-dive interview show about iOS and Mac development, design, and culture.
- [App Story](http://www.appstorypodcast.com) — Stories behind popular iOS apps.
- [Release Notes](https://releasenotes.tv/) — A podcast about the business of Apple software development.
- [Runtime](https://spec.fm/podcasts/runtime) — A show about Android and iOS development.
- [Consult](https://consultpodcast.com/#_=_) — A podcast by two indie developers about consulting and freelancing on Apple platforms.
- [Swift Playhouse](http://www.swiftplayhouse.com/) — Swift programming topics for beginners and experienced developers.

## Newsletters

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="iOS Dev Weekly" icon="mail" href="https://iosdevweekly.com/">
    A hand-picked round-up of the best iOS development links every week. One of the longest-running iOS newsletters. Free.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Swift Weekly Brief" icon="newspaper" href="https://swiftweekly.github.io/">
    A community-driven weekly newsletter about Swift.org. Curated by Jesse Squires and published free every Thursday.
  </Card>
  <Card title="iOS Goodies" icon="gift" href="https://ios-goodies.com">
    Weekly iOS newsletter covering articles, tools, and code. Concise and well-curated.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SwiftLee" icon="bolt" href="https://www.avanderlee.com/">
    A weekly blog about Swift, iOS, and Xcode tips and tricks by Antoine van der Lee.
  </Card>
  <Card title="iOS Cookies Newsletter" icon="cookie" href="https://us11.campaign-archive.com/home/?u=cd1f3ed33c6527331d82107ba&id=532dc7fb64">
    A weekly digest of new iOS libraries written in Swift.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Swift Developments" icon="code" href="https://andybargh.com/swiftdevelopments/">
    A weekly curated newsletter with hand-picked links, videos, tools and tutorials for iOS, WatchOS and AppleTV developers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="raywenderlich.com Weekly" icon="book-open" href="https://www.raywenderlich.com/newsletter">
    Sign up to receive the latest tutorials from raywenderlich.com each week. High quality editorial content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Server-Side Swift Weekly" icon="server" href="https://www.serverswift.tech">
    A weekly newsletter with the best links related to server-side Swift and cross-platform developer tools.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

More newsletters:

- [AwesomeiOS Weekly](http://weekly.awesomeios.com) — The official newsletter for the awesome-ios project.
- [iOS Dev Tools Weekly](https://iosdev.tools) — The greatest iOS development tools, including websites, desktop and mobile apps, and backend services.
- [Indie iOS Focus Weekly](http://indieiosfocus.com/) — Curated by Chris Beshore, focused on the best iOS dev links and tutorials beyond the usual news. Published every Thursday.
- [Mobile Developers Cafe](https://mobiledeveloperscafe.com) — A weekly newsletter for mobile developers with substantial iOS content.
- [Indie Watch](https://indie.watch/) — A weekly newsletter featuring the best apps made by indie iOS developers.
- [iOS Trivia Weekly](https://wanderbit.us4.list-manage.com/subscribe?u=4e20cd8ea3a0ce09ff4619a52&id=5898a5992b) — Three challenging questions about iOS development every Wednesday.

## Community websites and blogs

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="NSHipster" icon="globe" href="https://nshipster.com">
    A journal of the overlooked bits in Objective-C, Swift, and Cocoa. Detailed, opinionated articles by Mattt Thompson and others.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Objc.io" icon="book" href="https://www.objc.io/">
    In-depth, community-driven resources on iOS and macOS development. Home of the objc.io books and Swift Talk video series.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Point Free" icon="film" href="https://www.pointfree.co/">
    A video series exploring functional programming and Swift, hosted by Brandon Williams and Stephen Celis.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Natasha The Robot" icon="user" href="https://www.natashatherobot.com/">
    iOS development blog by Natasha Murashev with a focus on Swift and Swift evolution.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apple's Swift Blog" icon="apple" href="https://developer.apple.com/swift/blog/">
    Official announcements and deep-dives into the Swift language and standard library from Apple engineers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hacking With Swift" icon="code-2" href="https://www.hackingwithswift.com">
    Learn to code iPhone and iPad apps with tutorials by Paul Hudson. Includes the popular 100 Days of SwiftUI series.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
